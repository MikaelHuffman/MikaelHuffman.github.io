The Evo Wall Breakers are extraordinary. You use Wall Breakers on offense for repeated chip damage. You can play it in the middle to split them, forcing your opponent to overspend for defense. The Evo is guaranteed hit if your opponent doesn't have their building in cycle. Also great for defense, kiting, distracting, etc.

The Evolution Valkyrie is an incredible minitank good for offense, defense, and plain out support. Wall Breakers can push her and make her faster too.

The Magic Archer excels at piercing into other troops / the enemy tower for bonus damage. You can use this geometry to chip away at their buildings.

Mighty Miner is beyond incredible for defense. On top of that, you can put Wall Breakers behind him and toggle ability to go to the other lane.
